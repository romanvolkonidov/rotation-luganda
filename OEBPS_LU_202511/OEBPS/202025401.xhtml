7. “Omuntu Omugabi Ajja Kuweebwa Emikisa”

(Ddak. 15) Kukubaganya birowoozo. Kya kukubirizibwa mukadde.

Bwe tuwaayo ebiseera byaffe, amaanyi gaffe n’ebintu ebirala okusobola okuyamba abalala, tufuna emikisa mingi. Kya lwatu oyo gwe tuba tugabidde asanyuka. Kyokka naffe tusanyuka. (Nge 22:9) Tuba basanyufu kubanga tuba tukoppye Yakuwa era tufuna emikisa gye.—Nge 19:17; Yak 1:17.

  • Bafunye batya essanyu eriva mu kugaba?
