Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa Balina Amateeka ge Bagoberera Agakwata ku Kwogerezeganya?

Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa bakkiriza nti emisingi n’amateeka ebiri mu Bayibuli bisobola okubayamba okusalawo mu ngeri esanyusa Katonda era ebaganyula. (Isaaya 48:17, 18) Si ffe tweteerawo amateeka ago n’emisingi, naye tubigoberera. Weetegereze egimu ku misingi n’amateeka ebikwata ku kwogerezeganya.

● Obufumbo bwa lubeerera. (Matayo 19:6) Olw’okuba abantu abayingira obufumbo basooka kwogerezeganya, Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa okwogerezeganya bakutwala nga kukulu.

● Abo abakuze ekimala okusobola okuyingira obufumbo be basaanidde okwogerezeganya. Abantu abo baba bayise mu “kiseera ekya kabuvubuka,” oba ekiseera okwegomba okw’okwegatta we kubeerera okw’amaanyi.—1 Abakkolinso 7:36.

● Abo aboogereza oba aboogerezebwa basaanidde okuba nga tewali kibalemesa kuyingira bufumbo. Abantu abamu be bagattulula, tebasaanidde kuddamu kuyingira bufumbo, okusinziira ku mateeka ga Katonda. Ekigambo kye Bayibuli kiraga nti ensonga yokka abafumbo kwe balina okusinziira okugattululwa, bwe bwenzi.—Matayo 19:9.

● Abakristaayo abaagala okuyingira obufumbo basaanide kuwasa mu Mukama waffe mwokka. (1 Abakkolinso 7:39) Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa bakimanyi nti ekiragiro ekyo kikwata ku abo bokka ab’enzikiriza yaabwe era abaabatizibwa ng’Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa, so si abo abassa obussa ekitiibwa mu ebyo Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa bye bakkiririzaamu. (2 Abakkolinso 6:14) Okuva edda n’edda, Katonda azzenga alagira abaweereza be okuwasa oba okufumbirwa abo be bafaanaganya nabo enzikiriza. (Olubereberye 24:3; Malaki 2:11) Abanoonyereza bakirabye nti etteeka eryo likola.

● Buli Mujulirwa wa Yakuwa ye yeesalirawo okutandika okwogereza oba okwogerezebwa era n’ani gw’anaayogerezeganya naye. Ekyo kituukana n’omusingi ogugamba nti: “Buli omu ajja kwetikka obuvunaanyizibwa bwe.” (Abaggalatiya 6:5, obugambo obuli wansi) Wadde kiri kityo, bwe kituuka ku nsonga y’okwogerezeganya, bangi basaba Abajulirwa ba Yakuwa abakuze mu by’omwoyo okubawa ku magezi.—Engero 1:5.

ESSOMO 7

Ebituufu era Ebyesigika

Lukka 1:3

MU BUFUNZE: Wa obukakafu obwesigika okusobola okuyamba abakuwuliriza okutegeera ensonga gy’oyogerako.

ENGERI Y’OKUKIKOLAMU:

  • Noonyereza okuva mu nsonda ezeesigika. By’oyogera byesigamye ku Bayibuli, era gisomeemu butereevu bwe kiba kisoboka. Bw’okozesa bannassaayansi kye baazuula, ekyafulumira mu mawulire, ekyokulabirako, oba obukakafu obulala, kakasa nti ky’okozesezza kituufu.

  • By’onoonyerezza bikozese bulungi. Nnyonnyola ebyawandiikibwa mu ngeri ekwatagana n’ekiba ky’ogerwako mu nnyiriri eziriraanyeewo, mu ngeri ekwatagana n’obubaka obuli mu Bayibuli, era n’ebitabo ebikubibwa “omuddu omwesigwa.” (Mat. 24:45) Bw’okozesa by’ozudde mu kitabo ekitaakubibwa muddu mwesigwa, bikozese mu ngeri ekwatagana n’ekigendererwa ky’omuwandiisi.

    Bw’oba oyogera ku miwendo oba ku bukakafu bw’ozudde, tosavuwaza. Ng’ekyokulabirako “abantu abamu” baleme kufuuka “abantu abasinga obungi,” “oluusi” kireme kufuuka “ebiseera ebisinga,” oba “oyinza” kireme kufuuka “oteekwa.”

  • Yamba abakuwuliriza okufumiitiriza. Ng’omaze okusoma ekyawandiikibwa oba okuwa ekyokulabirako, buuza ebibuuzo mu ngeri ey’amagezi kiyambe abakuwuliriza bo bennyini okulaba ekituufu.

MU BUWEEREZA

Bw’oba weeteekateeka okugenda okubuulira, lowooza ku bibuuzo abantu bye bayinza okukubuuza era onoonyereze ku ngeri gy’oyinza okubiddamu. Omuntu bw’akubuuza ekibuuzo nga tomanyi kya kuddamu, mugambe nti ojja kusooka okinoonyerezeeko, oddeyo okiddemu.

ESSOMO 36

Yefusa Asuubiza Yakuwa

Abayisirayiri baddamu okuva ku Yakuwa ne basinza bakatonda ab’obulimba. Abaamoni bwe baalumba Abayisirayiri, bakatonda abo ab’obulimba tebaayamba Bayisirayiri. Abayisirayiri baabonaabona okumala emyaka mingi. N’ekyavaamu, baagamba Yakuwa nti: ‘Twonoonye mu maaso go. Tukwegayiridde, tununule mu mukono gw’abalabe baffe.’ Abayisirayiri baasaanyaawo ebifaananyi bye baali basinza ne baddamu okusinza Yakuwa. Yakuwa yawulira nga takyayagala kulaba Bayisirayiri nga babonaabona.

Omusajja omulwanyi ayitibwa Yefusa ye yalondebwa okukulembera Abayisirayiri okulwanyisa Abaamoni. Yefusa yagamba Yakuwa nti: ‘Bw’onootuyamba okuwangula olutalo luno, bwe nnaddayo eka, nja kukuwa omuntu anaasooka okufuluma mu nnyumba yange okunkulisaayo.’ Yakuwa yaddamu okusaba kwa Yefusa era yamuyamba okuwangula olutalo olwo.

Yefusa bwe yakomawo awaka, omuntu eyasooka okufuluma mu nnyumba okumukulisaayo yali muwala we, omwana omu yekka gwe yalina. Yafuluma ng’azina era ng’akuba akagoma. Kiki Yefusa kye yakola? Okusobola okukituukiriza, nnina okukusindika ogende oweereze ku weema entukuvu mu Siiro.’ Naye muwala we yamugamba nti: ‘Taata, bw’oba nga wasuubiza Yakuwa, tuukiriza kye wasuubiza. Kyokka nkusaba ekintu kimu; ka nsooke ŋŋende mu nsozi okumala emyezi ebiri mbeereko wamu n’abawala mikwano gyange, oluvannyuma ŋŋende e Siiro.’ Muwala wa Yefusa yaweereza Yakuwa n’obwesigwa ku weema entukuvu obulamu bwe bwonna. ESSOMO 37

Yakuwa Ayogera ne Samwiri

Batabani be abo baali tebagondera mateeka ga Yakuwa era baayisanga bubi nnyo abantu. Abayisirayiri bwe baaleetanga ebiweebwayo eri Yakuwa, Kofuni ne Fenekaasi beggirangako ennyama esinga obulungi ne bagitwala. Eli yawulira ebyo batabani be bye baali bakola naye talina kye yakolawo. Naye ddala Yakuwa yandikkirizza embeera eno okweyongera mu maaso?

Wadde nga Samwiri yali muto nnyo ku Kofuni ne Fenekaasi, teyakoppa nneeyisa yaabwe embi. Ekyo kyasanyusa nnyo Yakuwa. Lumu ekiro, Samwiri bwe yali yeebase, yawulira nga waliwo amuyita. Yasituka n’agenda eri Eli, n’amugamba nti: ‘Nzuuno!’ Naye Eli yamugamba nti: ‘Sinnakuyita. Ddayo weebake.’ Samwiri yaddayo ne yeebaka. Ekyo kyabaawo emirundi emirala ebiri. Ku mulundi ogw’okusatu, Eli yakitegeera nti Yakuwa ye yali ayita Samwiri. Eli yagamba Samwiri nti bw’addamu okuwulira eddoboozi eryo, agambe nti: ‘Yogera Yakuwa, kubanga omuweereza wo awulira.’

Samwiri yaddayo ne yeebaka. Oluvannyuma yawulira eddoboozi nga ligamba nti: ‘Samwiri! Samwiri!’ Yaddamu nti: ‘Yogera, kubanga omuweereza wo awulira.’ Yakuwa yagamba Samwiri nti: ‘Gamba Eli nti ŋŋenda kumubonereza awamu n’ab’ennyumba ye. Akimanyi nti batabani be bakola ebintu ebibi ku weema yange entukuvu naye talina ky’akolawo.’ Obudde bwe bwakya, Samwiri yaggulawo enzigi za weema entukuvu nga bwe yakolanga bulijjo. Kyokka yatya okubuulira Eli ebyo Yakuwa bye yali agambye. Naye Eli yamuyita n’amubuuza nti: ‘Mwana wange, Yakuwa yakugambye ki? Tonkweka kintu kyonna.’ Bwe kityo, Samwiri yabuulira Eli buli kimu.

Samwiri yeeyongera okukula era Yakuwa yeeyongera okuba naye. Abantu mu ggwanga lya Isirayiri lyonna baali bakimanyi nti Yakuwa yali alonze Samwiri okuba nnabbi era omulamuzi.
